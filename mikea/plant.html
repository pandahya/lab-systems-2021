Plant

Level 5 Green Smart Plant

Plant is a smart houseplant with impeccable craftsmanship for a clean design and intuitive controls for easy maintenance. Using state of the art technology, Plant purifies the air of 95% of known pollutants.
It's easily pairable with the latest microchips to notify you with customizable notifications about its needs, your air quality, how it's doing, and more.

Size: 3 unitsq x 3 unitsq x 5 unitsq
PlantOS version 1.45.9
Compatible with microchips Grade B and above.
Which microchip do I have?

As mandated by Policy no. 88590 to reduce carbon emissions, each household must have one plant for every two people, each plant must be a Level 5 Green/Blue/White or higher.
Plant is a Level 5 Green product.
